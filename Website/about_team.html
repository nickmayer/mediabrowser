About Media Browser Team

Media Browser is an open source project that has a core team of senior developers (listed below) and other team members who contribute regularly to the project. We have a growing community of registered forum members from all around the world, with a passion that is unrivaled.

Senior Developers

Contribute to the Community

Being an Open Source Software project, you and anyone else who uses Media Browser are more than welcome to help us make it better! There are many different ways that you and your friends can help contribute to the project. Some ways to contribute:

  • Code (C# / MCML Programming-Language Development)
  • Wiki Citizens (update our public wiki with documentation, how to's, FAQs, etc.)
  • Help Teach (provide assistance to other Media Browser users on the forum)
  • Bug Reports (help find bugs and report them on the forum)
  • Graphics (create icons, web graphics, and other promotional material)

 

Thank You

On behalf of the Media Browser Team we'd like to thank everyone who

  • Submitted bug reports to make a higher quality product
  • Submitted feature requests and suggestions to help us improve our software
  • Submitted patches to help us improve and fix bugs in our software

We would like to also take this opportunity to extend a special thanks to all the developers and testers of this project for putting their time and effort into making Media Browser a great plug-in for Media Center.
